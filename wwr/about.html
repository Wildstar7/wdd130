McKenzie Willamette Whitewater Warriors

Conquer the rapids, claim the glory! Here in the beautiful wilderness of the Pacific Northwest, adventure is more than just a pastime - it's a lifestyle. Whether you're a seasoned warrior or a new recruit, our experienced guides are well-equipped to guide you from roaring rapids, to serene off-channels, through the most awe-inspiring vistas the PNW has to offer. Are you ready to plunge into the journey of a lifetime?

History

Noah, Benjamin, Levi, and Wyatt are all Oregon natives. While growing up in this Pacific wonderland, the four brothers became especially fond of rafting the exciting and enchanting rivers of the Pacific Northwest.

One day, while rafting together, Noah said, "You know, why don't we become river guides?" The brothers made a pact and decided to pursue their lifelong passion - McKenzie Willamette Whitewater Warriors was born!
